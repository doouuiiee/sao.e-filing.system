SAO E-Record Filing System

The Student Affairs Office (SAO) E-Record Filing System is a comprehensive digital platform designed specifically for Cordova Catholic School Multipurpose Cooperative to streamline student record management, attendance tracking, and disciplinary action handling.

Our system provides a secure, efficient, and user-friendly interface for students, teachers, and SAO coordinators to manage academic records, monitor attendance patterns, schedule appointments, and maintain comprehensive documentation.

With automated email notifications, real-time updates, and detailed reporting capabilities, we ensure that all stakeholders stay informed and connected throughout the academic journey.
